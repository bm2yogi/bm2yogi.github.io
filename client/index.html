What is HabitCracker?

Every year, over 108 million Americans diet and lose weight, only to gain it back. Over 220,000 Americans undergo weight loss surgery because they want to be more healthy. In their efforts to lose weight, Americans spend over $20 billion dollars a year on things that just don't work.

HabitCracker is different. Rather than a one-size fits all approach to fixing the symptoms of bad habits, HabitCracker starts with you and learns from you to help you break free from bad habits and develop and maintain lasting, meaningful change.

HabitCracker is not for everyone. It might be for you. If you give HabitCracker a shot, and it doesn't change anything for you, we'll give you your money back.
